A Little Bit About Me

Hi, I’m Zach!

I'm a 17 year old highschool student from Canada who enjoys digital visual arts, coding, web design, math, physics, and chemistry. In my free time I grow both terrestrial and aquatic plants. I plan to pursue digital arts, science, and math in my post secondary education as well as into my career. I’m currently in DMA 12, because I enjoy digital visual arts and coding and this course allows me to extend my knowledge in both of those areas.

University: UBC

When curiosity and drive fuel a vision for a better world, the University of British Columbia opens doors to opportunity. A world-leading center of teaching, learning and research excellence, UBC transforms personal initiative into innovation, and new ideas into impact. UBC supports inspired students, staff and faculty on their journey of discovery, and challenges them to realize their greatest potential.

UBC’s two main campuses are situated in Vancouver and in Kelowna in the Okanagan Valley. Also in Vancouver, UBC Robson Square is a vibrant learning center in the heart of downtown, the UBC Learning Exchange is a community engagement initiative based in Vancouver’s Downtown Eastside, and UBC’s Centre for Digital Media at the Great Northern Way Campus is located in Mount Pleasant.

Program: BMS

BMS Page

Work alongside Visual Arts and Digital Media professors in a close-knit learning cohort, which integrates creativity and research into a variety of areas such as creative technologies, multimedia platforms, indigenous media arts, art history, visual culture, and the digital humanities. Students will have the opportunity to take a minor or enroll in a range of media-related courses across disciplines, including visual and digital arts, data science, cultural studies, creative writing, history, and digital media.
